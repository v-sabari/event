## [Unreleased]

### Added
- Initial public release of the Campus Connect frontend.
- Role-based dashboards for Student, Student Organizer, Faculty Coordinator/HOD, and Super Admin.
- Event discovery, calendar view, creation/editing, roster, and attendance workflows.
- Venue, department, club, and event-category management screens.
- Reports and notifications.
- Command palette for quick navigation.
- Centralized Axios client with automatic access-token refresh on 401 responses.
- Route-level guards mirroring backend role authorization rules.
